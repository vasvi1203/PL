Objectives

  • To make students conversant with basic Biology regarding the life processes.
  • To impart knowledge about the common corridors of biology and engineering as biologically inspired technologies like designs in nature, bioenergetics, bioprocesses, biomaterials, biomechanics, bioimaging, bioinformatics, bioinstrumentation etc.
  • To introduce recent trends in biology viz. genetic & tissue engineering, stem cell engineering, bio and nanotechnology etc. with the objective of appreciating engineering principles in biological systems.
